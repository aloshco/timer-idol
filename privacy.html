Privacy Policy — Timer Idol

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Timer Idol ("the app", "we") is a collection of party games. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum. This policy explains what is collected and why.

Information we do NOT collect

The app has no accounts or sign-in. We do not ask for, collect, or store your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or location.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep the app free, we show ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect a device advertising identifier (such as the IDFA) and basic usage data. On iOS, this identifier is only used for tracking if you allow it through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, you still see ads, but they are non-personalized. Pro users see no ads at all. See how Google uses data from apps that use its services.

Analytics

We use aggregate, non-identifying usage data (such as which games are opened) to understand how the app is used and improve it. This is not linked to your identity.

In-app purchase

The optional "Timer Idol Pro" purchase is handled entirely by Apple. We never receive or store your payment details.

Children

Timer Idol is a general-audience game and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can reset or limit ad tracking any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Users in regions such as the EEA and certain US states are shown a consent choice for advertising.

Contact

Questions? Email mohammad.sami.apps@gmail.com.
